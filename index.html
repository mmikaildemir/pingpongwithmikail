Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common coaching questions

What's the typical session length?

Sessions are 50 minutes.

What times are available?

You can schedule sessions on Thursdays from 5pm to 10pm and Sundays from 12pm to 5pm.

Do I need my own racket?

A professional level racket is provided, though regular players may want to eventually invest in their own paddle.

How do I schedule sessions?

Contact me directly from the contact button below.

What about pricing options?

We offer flexible plans based on your training needs. Start with a free 20-minute introductory course! Contact me for detailed pricing information.

What type of shoes should I wear?

Any non-slip, soft-soled shoes are great.
